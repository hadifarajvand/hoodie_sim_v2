## 1. Objective

Prepare a bounded comparison between the legacy/simplified 3D/3-action campaign path and the paper_default 74D/22-action bounded pilot path.

## 2. Scientific Purpose

Produce first comparable Phase 1 metrics before figure reproduction or full training, establishing a measurable baseline for the 3D/3A legacy against the 74D/22A paper-faithful implementation.

## 3. Comparison Scope

- Bounded only
- Max 3 episodes
- Max 50 slots per episode
- No full training
- No figure reproduction
- No Phase 2 / DCQ-MADRL

## 7. Acceptance Criteria

- Both paths execute bounded runs (3 episodes x 50 slots each)
- Both produce comparable JSON/MD summaries under `artifacts/analysis/bounded-baseline-comparison/`
- No NaN/Inf losses for either path
- No illegal actions for either path
- `full_campaign_enabled` remains false for both paths
- Legacy 3D/3A metrics surface for side-by-side comparison

## 8. Risks

- Metrics may not be scientifically meaningful yet because the 50-slot horizon is short relative to the full campaign (thousands of slots)
- Legacy 3D/3A and paper 74D/22A differ in state/action dimensionality and may not be directly performance-comparable at bounded scale
- `pending_at_horizon_count` may dominate for both paths due to the tight 50-slot bound, making completion metrics difficult to interpret
- The comparison harness may need different trainer/config parameters per path, increasing implementation complexity
