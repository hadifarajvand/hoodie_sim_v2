# HOODIE Resource Log

## Authority Levels

- Source of truth: `resources/papers/hoodie/original/HOODIE_paper.pdf`
- Supporting references: `resources/papers/hoodie/ocr/merged.md`, `resources/papers/hoodie/ocr/merged.txt`
- Non-authoritative implementation references: simulator repositories under `resources/references/simulators/`

## Usage Rules

- The original PDF governs any paper-derived decision.
- OCR files may support reading and search, but they do not override the PDF.
- Simulator references may inform structure, naming, and implementation patterns only.
- Simulator references must not be treated as behavioral truth for the paper reproduction.
